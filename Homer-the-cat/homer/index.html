Homer

About Homer

Homer was adopted by Sarah in January of 2017. He was found in the parking-lot of a MacDonalds on a very cold night. He loves to eat, sleep, and play with his jingle toys. Until recently, Homer lived with his pal Daisy, a playful Shitzu mix the Sarah's family adopted earlier. Homer is now making Indianapolis his new home and can't wait to make new friends.

Characteristics

  1. Being sassy in the morning, before his owners have had coffee.
  2. Sleeping on the guest bed for his afternoon naps.
  3. Chowing down on Fancy Feast and Blue Buffalo.
  4. Playing with toys and scratching on couches.
  5. Going for morning walks around the yard.
  6. Meowing.... a lot......
